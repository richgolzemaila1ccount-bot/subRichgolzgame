Finding the Sword RG001





You decide not to pray.




And then, you feel a kind of numbness.



It is not as if something has been added to you, nor taken away from you.



You do not feel empty. Nothing seems to be missing.




And yet, you feel a sensation without sensation—



something without meaning.



Nothing at all.







You decided to go home.









You go straight home.


Once you arrive, you pause to think.


Perhaps—


Perhaps, if you had prayed,


you might have received something good.


And then—


You wonder if you should try the journey again.
