Finding the Sword RG001



       The stone you carry is, in truth, the legendary core.
This stone is given only to the one who truly deserves it.

That core is the sword itself.
In other words, you had already obtained the sword from the very beginning.

Yet the reason this journey is called an adventure
is that the one who receives the stone must pass through three trials alongside it
in order to truly obtain the legendary sword—
a sword no one else can ever claim.

The first and most important condition to obtain the legendary sword
is to desire it by your own will.

Because you chose to pray of your own free will,
you were chosen at that moment,
and the stone was placed in your hands.

(You feel a deep sense of fulfillment.
You realize you had it from the very beginning.)
(Your prayer had already been answered.)

The second trial is love.

If, in your life, you have ever loved another person,
then I will be shown to me.

Love is beautiful in and of itself.

(You feel a deep sense of fulfillment.)
(As great as the love that person gave you,
so too is the depth of the fulfillment you now feel.)

The third trial is wisdom.

Near this place lies a river—
the closest, the deepest, and the most magnificent of all.
It is both a river and a sea.

Its depths are immense.
Perhaps so deep that simply looking into it feels frightening.

Wash the sword within that river.

(You feel a deep sense of fulfillment.
A vast sea.
A profound river.)

Now, do you understand?

Heat says.
