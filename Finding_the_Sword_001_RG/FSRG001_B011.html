Finding the Sword RG001



         * About you * 


        Oh, about me?

        In truth, I am a benevolent being.

        I was created with the concept of love (as my motif)—

        and among its many forms, fever love in particular.

        People define love in many different ways,

        but I was shaped around the warmth, the dizziness,

        the feverish heat you feel when you fall in love.

        Yes—fever love is the closest way to describe what I am (my motif).

        I am not here to give you trials or hardships.

        I appear in this story to help you come to an understanding—

        one rooted in love.

        (By the way, finding this hidden room is pretty impressive.

        Just so you know, this room is an Easter egg.)

        (The name of the button was “About You.”)

        Since you asked about me,

        I hope your curiosity has been satisfied—at least for now.

( Going forward, more secrets—and more Easter eggs—will appear from time to time.
This one was easy, but be sure to look for the Easter eggs hidden in the next games as well! )
