Finding the Sword RG001



        You realize that the being before you is Heat.

        Heat is the sun—
        and yet, at the same time, only a part of it.

        In this world, the sun chose to send down a fragment of itself—
        something closer to a shadow than a true reflection—
        to aid you on your journey.

        Perhaps many of the chosen ones before you,
        and those who will come after,
        also forged their swords with Heat’s guidance.

        Or perhaps you alone are the true hero—
        and what we call legend was nothing more than
        a carefully crafted, meaningful guidebook,
        created to lead you to this very place.

        You instinctively sense that farewell is approaching.

        The being who wore the face of the one you loved most,
        who granted you understanding,
        and who guided your journey—

        Heat prepares to fulfill its final task.

        Before long, Heat returns to its original form:
        a small, crimson sphere, round and no larger than your fist.

        It drifts down near your hands.

        And then—

        Resonating with your core,
        Heat masterfully forges it into a sword.

        Congratulations.
        You have obtained the sword.

        Now, you head to the river
        to cleanse the blade.
